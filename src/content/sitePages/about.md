designer, tech repair main, family IT support guy, and everything else.

So that's where the "Scally Network" site came from: I couldn't ever keep track of all this stuff I
do and share it without a personal stash for everything.

I first and foremost want to keep this for myself, but hopefully others will view this and see what
I do, maybe learn something, or even just enjoy some pictures.
